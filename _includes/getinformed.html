Must Reads

A selection of highly readable books offering in-depth insight into the multiple threats facing the Global Ocean today:

  • Charles Clover - The End of the Line (Ebury Press - 2005)
  • Sylvia Earle - The World is Blue: How our fate and the oceans are one (National Geographic Society 2010)
  • Callum Roberts - The Unnatural History of the Sea (Island Press - 2009)
  • Callum Roberts - The Ocean of Life (Penguin Books - 2013)
  • Alex Rogers - The Deep: The Hidden Wonders of Our Oceans and How We Can Protect Them (Headline Publishing Group - 2019)

  • George Monibot - Stop eating fish. It's the only way to save the life in our seas (The Guardian, 9 May 2019)
  • The Economist - Getting serious about overfishing (The Economist, 27 May 2017)
